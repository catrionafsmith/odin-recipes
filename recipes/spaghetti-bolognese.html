Spaghetti Bolognese Recipe

Spaghetti bolognese is a dish which comes from the Bologna area of Italy. It is a meat ragu, which is actually traditionally served with tagliatelle or other wide, flat pasta shapes.

Spaghetti bolognese can also be prepared using meat substitutes to reduce your carbon impact.

Ingredients

  • Minced beef or meat substitute
  • Chopped onion
  • Crushed tomoatoes
  • Tomato paste
  • Black pepper
  • Spaghetti

Steps

  1. Brown the mince
  2. Add the onion and cook until translucent
  3. Drain any fat
  4. Add the tomato paste and chopped tomatoes
  5. Simmer for 1 hour
  6. Cook spaghetti until al dente
  7. Serve and enjoy!
