Smoked Salmon Recipe

Smoked salmon is a delicious dish that can be enjoyed any time of the day or night. It is often eaten on a bagel, on sliced brown bread, or just by hand.

The best salmon are hand-caught from the Tay river in Scotland, especially around the area where the Isla and the Ericht meet the Tay.

Ingredients

  • Sliced Scottish smoked salmon
  • Lemon
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Take the salmon out of its packaging
  2. Cut a slice of lemon and squeeze it over the salmon
  3. Grind black pepper over the salmon
